# Glossary

## Overview

This glossary defines technical terms, abbreviations, and concepts used throughout the TennisLabAI project.

The objective is to ensure consistent terminology across documentation, source code, experiments, and scientific publications.

---

# A

## AI

Artificial Intelligence.

Computational methods that enable machines to perform tasks requiring human intelligence.

---

# B

## Bland–Altman Analysis

A statistical method used to evaluate agreement between two measurement techniques.

---

# C

## Computer Vision

A field of artificial intelligence that enables computers to interpret visual information from images and videos.

---

# D

## DOF

Degree of Freedom.

The number of independent movements available to a joint or mechanical system.

---

# F

## Feature Extraction

The process of deriving meaningful variables from raw sensor or image data for analysis or machine learning.

---

# G

## GNN

Graph Neural Network.

A neural network architecture designed to process graph-structured data, including human skeleton representations.

---

# I

## ICC

Intraclass Correlation Coefficient.

A statistical metric used to evaluate reliability or agreement between measurement methods.

---

## IK

Inverse Kinematics.

The process of estimating joint angles from observed body segment positions.

---

## IMU

Inertial Measurement Unit.

A wearable sensor that measures acceleration, angular velocity, and orientation.

---

# K

## Kalman Filter

A recursive algorithm for estimating the state of a dynamic system from noisy measurements.

---

# M

## Markerless Motion Capture

Motion capture performed without reflective markers, typically using computer vision.

---

## MAE

Mean Absolute Error.

A metric representing the average absolute difference between predicted and reference values.

---

## Muscle Activation

The level of muscle engagement estimated through biomechanical simulation or measured using EMG.

---

# O

## OpenSim

An open-source musculoskeletal simulation platform used for biomechanical analysis.

---

# P

## Pose Estimation

The process of estimating body joint locations from images or videos.

---

# R

## RMSE

Root Mean Square Error.

A common metric for evaluating prediction accuracy.

---

## ROM

Range of Motion.

The angular displacement available at a joint.

---

# S

## Sensor Fusion

The process of combining measurements from multiple sensors to improve estimation accuracy.

---

## SfM

Structure-from-Motion.

A computer vision technique for reconstructing 3D structure from multiple images.

---

# T

## Time Synchronization

The alignment of data collected from multiple sensors based on a common temporal reference.

---

# V

## Vision Transformer (ViT)

A transformer-based deep learning architecture designed for image understanding tasks.

---

# Future Updates

This glossary will expand as new terminology is introduced during the development of TennisLabAI.
